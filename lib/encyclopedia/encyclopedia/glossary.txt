## Story Specific
Tombakashi - Les Catabombs De Ceramqiue Dijitaru - Wasei-Eigo of "Tombeau De
Okaeshi", a tomb for returnining favor to undead kings. When an Ursinehomme
dies, the ground beneath them distorts into a treachorous obstacle course,
with the spirits of dead Bear-Men surving as barriers to ransacking the
comrade's corpse. Such creatures are often cybernetically enhanced,
such defenders often chosen from comrades whom had previously died in
battle.
