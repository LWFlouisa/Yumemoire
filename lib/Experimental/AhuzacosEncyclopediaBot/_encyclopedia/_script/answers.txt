Ahusacos is a pidgeon language that evolved out of the interaction between the French and Japanese languages. Originally more like Francais, it gradually developed its own spelling for French word classes and personal pronouns, and introducing vocabulary from Nihongo.
The national flag of the Ahusacos is a Japanese imperial circle placed in the middle of a French tricolor.
The national colors of the ahusacos is Red, Blue, and White. And sometimes red-puple.
